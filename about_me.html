About Me

My name is Hritheekka and I am a fourth year Electrical Engineering student at McMaster University. I am passionate about engineering and I am looking to work in either software or electrical positions. I would eventually like to work in IT or project management and get my P.Eng in the future. Aside from this, I am passionate about the arts, I am an Indian Classical Dancer, a musician learning to play both the piano and guitar, as well as an Indian Classical Carnatic Singer.

My goal is to bridge the gap between technology and the healthcare industry. I would like to apply my skills and experience to develop and implement solutions to pressing healthcare issues around the world, while focussing on the importance of sustainablility. Our focus on sustainability should be implemented in our daily work as we enter a time when we are aware of the significance of the environment and the need to preserve it. During my undergraduate years I would like to gain the technical skills required to be able to not only work in these fields but be an integral member in the community, and I know I can gain the appropriate guidance and education through co-op positions and other work experience.

Outside of my education, I co-founded the Student Chapter of HandiCare Intl, a registered Canadian charitable organization that helps disadvantaged youth across Southern India and Canada. Our team hosts in-person and virtual events that have raised over $1500, while encouraging youth to get involved!
